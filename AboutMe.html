My Biography


1.My life before going to university.
2.My life after going to university

1.My name is Kaiyrbek Nurali.I am 17 years old. I was born in the city of Zaisan. But I spent most of my childhood in the village called Novostroyka. My grandparents lived there. Almost every summer I came to the village during the summer holidays. I went to school at the age of 6, studied well, I finished 10 classes in Ust-Kamenogorsk. I finished the 11th grade in Astana. I am a calm person, I love silence. I like to solve rubik's cubes, play chess, and also watch hockey.

2.After school, I decided to enter a university where there is an IT specialty. Why? At school, mathematics was the closest subject to me. Therefore, I decided that an IT specialty would suit me. I passed the UNT 2 times for a grant. Both times I scored 91 points. I entered the Astana IT University on a grant and now I am studying cybersecurity here.
